EduCoin



At the core of our financial strategy is EduCoins, a digital cryptocurrency. Rural girls earn EduCoins through various meaningful activities: by showcasing their creativity and entrepreneurial spirit through the sale of their locally crafted goods on our digital marketplace and even through rewards for consistent attendance and academic excellence.

These EduCoins hold real value, directly offsetting educational expenses like fees and materials. Hence, they not only address affordability but also incentivize engagement in education and community, cultivating crucial life skills and contributing to the holistic development of these young women for a more equitable future.
